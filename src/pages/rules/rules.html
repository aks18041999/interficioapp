INSTRUCTIONS INSTRUCTIONS

  • The murder mystery consists of 21 levels, each having a main question and a hint.
  • Hint questions revolve around your very own marked warrior. On solving these hint questions, a main question will appear which will point to a location within this campus.
  • In order to cross a level, you have to reach the correct location and press submit.
  • Commencing on the 2nd, this war will last for three days, with 7 unsolved levels being released each day.
